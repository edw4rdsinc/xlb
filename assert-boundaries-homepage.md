## Who I Am Section

**Section Headline:**
Meet Pauline

**Intro Quote:**
"I specialize in trauma, hoarding disorder, and clinical supervision for therapists of color — because everyone deserves to be truly seen and supported in their healing journey."

**Body Copy:**
Hi, I'm Dr. Pauline Botchway, a licensed therapist serving Oregon and Washington. I bring PhD-level expertise in hoarding disorder and years of experience working with adults navigating trauma, boundary issues, and life transitions.

What sets my practice apart is cultural attunement. I recognize and honor the unique experiences of people of color, providing a safe space where you can be your authentic self without judgment.

**Four Pillars of My Practice:**

- **Licensed Therapist** — Professional credentials ensuring trusted, ethical care (OR & WA)
- **PhD-Level Expertise** — Specialized training in hoarding disorder, offering clinically sound guidance
- **Culturally Attuned Support** — Deeply honoring the unique experiences of people of color
- **Nonjudgmental Guidance** — Compassionate support creating a safe space for healing and growth
